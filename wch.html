About this place

Nestled in the heart of Blue Ridge, Georgia, Weeks Creek Haven is a thoughtfully curated mountain retreat. This authentic chinking log cabin sits streamside in the Aska Adventure Area — owned and cared for by H & L Havens. Every detail reflects a commitment to providing a true sanctuary where guests can unplug and immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the North Georgia mountains.

Wake up to the sound of the creek, spend your evenings on the porch swing listening to the rain, and gather everyone around the fire pit under the trees. Whether you're here to hike the ridges, cast a line on the Toccoa River, or simply exhale — this is your cabin away from home.
