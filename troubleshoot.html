Fireplace & Gas Logs

STOP! If you smell gas, get everyone out and call Lance: 770.549.9358

To Start: Slowly turn gas knob to 'ON'. Burner should light gently.

If Pilot is Out: Turn to 'PILOT', push in, and hold lit match to back right prongs for 30-60s.
