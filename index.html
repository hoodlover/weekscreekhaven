Rustic Charm meets Mountain Comfort

Weeks Creek Haven (421 Weeks Creek Rd) is a unique chinking log cabin nestled in the Aska Adventure Area of Blue Ridge, GA. We blend the authentic feel of a traditional log home with high-speed Gigabit Fiber Internet, streamside peace, and a massive 98" screen. Perfect for a family getaway or a quiet mountain retreat.
