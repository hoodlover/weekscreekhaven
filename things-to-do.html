Hiking & Waterfalls

Trail Highlights

Swinging Bridge: Longest swinging bridge east of the Mississippi (270 ft).

Fall Branch Falls: Short, beginner-friendly double-waterfall hike.

Aska Trail System: 17 miles of multi-use trails for all skill levels.

Long Branch Loop: 2.3-mile moderate loop with a historic chimney.

Family Fun & River Adventures

Activities & Outings

Blue Ridge Railway: A 26-mile trip to the sister towns of McCaysville and Copperhill.

Mercier Orchards: Quintessential orchard famous for fried apple pies and cider.

Toccoa River Canoe Trail: A 13.8-mile trail through rapids and trout streams.

Lake Fun: Swimming at Morganton Point and boat rentals at Blue Ridge Pier.

Local Dining & Favorites

Provisions & Dining

Toccoa Riverside Restaurant: Famed for river views and fresh trout.

Iron Bridge Cafe: General store charm with breakfast and fishing gear.

Van Zandt’s: A local favorite for burgers and breakfast right around the corner.
