Before You Head Out...

Linens & Laundry: Strip used beds and place in laundry area. Please start at least one load.

Kitchen: Load and start the dishwasher. Clean out fridge items and wipe down sinks.

Trash: Place food trash in the Bear Proof Bin and non-food trash in the porch box.

Fireplace: Ensure gas logs are OFF (leaving the pilot on). Close the screens.

Hot Tub: Secure the cover and lower temp to 80°F.

Lock Up: Close all windows and ensure all three smart doors are locked.
