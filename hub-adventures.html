☑ Don't Forget to Pack

Mountain weather is wonderfully unpredictable — pack layers, sturdy shoes, and a swimsuit for the hot tub or river. Stock up on anything you need while still in downtown Blue Ridge — cell service fades fast once you head into the Aska Adventure Area. And don't sleep on it: Blue Ridge is the Trout Capital of Georgia. A fishing license might be the best thing in your bag.

▲ Outdoor & Hiking

🎒

Backpack

Small daypack for water, snacks, and trail gear. The trails here earn it.

👞

Footwear

Sturdy, broken-in hiking boots or trail shoes. The mountains don't care about fresh kicks.

🦩

Layers

Moisture-wicking tees, a fleece or hoodie for cool evenings, and a windbreaker or rain poncho. Blue Ridge weather keeps you guessing.

👔

Accessories

Sunscreen (SPF 15+), sunglasses, bug spray, and a hat. The sun is no joke at elevation.

⛸

Trekking Poles

Helpful on steep or uneven terrain — don't feel weird about them, they work.

🔦

Safety

Flashlight or headlamp plus a small first aid kit. Cell service is spotty out here — be ready.

📷

Camera

Your phone is fine, but the waterfalls and creek light deserve better. Bring the real one if you've got it.

☺ Relax Mode

(Yes — relaxing is absolutely an activity. A serious one.)

👔

Comfortable Clothes

Soft layers, slippers, and if you have a robe — bring it. No judgment here.

🔥

Cozy Extras

Extra blankets, firewood, and everything you need for s'mores. The firepit is waiting.

🎱

Entertainment

Board games, cards, a book — or just the sound of the creek. That counts.

🍳

Food

Eat out and explore the local spots, or do some proper home-style cooking at the cabin. The kitchen is fully stocked.

🛍

Reusable Shopping Bag

Downtown Blue Ridge has great shops and markets. You'll want to carry something home.

🏄

Water Activities

Water shoes for tubing or kayaking the Toccoa River — and a swimsuit for the hot tub when you get back.

🍻

Town & Dining

Casual clothes and comfortable shoes for Main Street — shops, restaurants, and a few really good local breweries.

✌

The Right Attitude

You don't really need much else. Come up here, slow down, and let the mountains do their thing.
