☀️ Deck Infrared Heater

Our deck is equipped with a **Briza Carbon Infrared Heater**. Unlike propane heaters, this uses sun-like rays to warm you directly, even in a mountain breeze!

To Start: Plug into the heavy-duty outlet on the deck and use the [Remote/Power Button] to turn on. You will feel heat within seconds.

Built-in Timer: You can set a timer from 1–9 hours so you don't have to worry about forgetting to turn it off.

Safety Features: The unit has a built-in "Tip-Over" sensor that automatically cuts power if it's knocked over.

Pro-Tip:

The metal sheath is designed to stay safe to the touch, but please keep blankets, towels, and furniture at least 3 feet away from the front of the heater!
