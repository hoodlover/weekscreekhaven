The Setting

Steps from Weeks Creek

Wake up to the sound of rushing water. The cabin sits right on Weeks Creek — grab a chair, cast a line, or just let the current do the talking. You're in the Aska Adventure Area, one of the most sought-after corners of North Georgia.

  • Private creek frontage — no neighbors, no noise
  • Fire pit and outdoor rocker porch
  • Large soaking hot tub overlooking the treeline
  • Hammock spot in the trees
98" 4K Smart TV

Inside the Cabin

Rustic Charm, Modern Comfort

Genuine chinking-log construction meets everything you actually need. Gigabit fiber, a massive smart TV, cozy beds for up to 11, and a kitchen stocked to cook real meals. It looks like the 1800s — it works like 2026.

  • Gigabit fiber WiFi throughout — password: creeklife
  • 98-inch 4K smart TV in the living room
  • Alexa voice control in every room
  • Full kitchen with all the essentials
  • Bidet in the master bath (yes, really)
