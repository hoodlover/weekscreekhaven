Mountain Safety

Garbage Safety: Use the 60-gallon bear-proof bins on the front porch. Keep bungee cords fastened securely.

Firepit: Located near the creek. Please ensure the fire is completely out before heading in for the night.

Wildlife: Don't feed the wildlife or leave loose garbage outside. There are black bears, coyotes, and deer in the area.
