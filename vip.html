Departure Checklist

  • • Strip sheets from the beds used.
  • • Wash sheets, pillow cases, and towels.
  • • Start the dryer before you head out.
  • • Hand wash dishes or start the dishwasher.
  • • Turn fireplace to 'Pilot'.
  • • Bag and take out all trash.
  • • Clean out fridge items.
  • • Turn off all lights.
  • • Lock 3 smart doors or say "Alexa, Going Home".
