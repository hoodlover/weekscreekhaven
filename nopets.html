Strict No-Pet Policy

To maintain a clean and allergen-free environment for all our guests, **Weeks Creek Haven is a strictly pet-free property.** We appreciate your cooperation in keeping our mountain retreat pristine.

⚠️ Unauthorized Pet Fee

Any evidence of a pet on the premises (including porches, decks, or stairs) will result in an immediate **$250.00 fine.**

Additional charges may apply for physical damage to the property or furniture.
