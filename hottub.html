How to Use the Cover Lifter

1

UNLOCK

Unlock and prepare the cover for lifting.

2

FOLD

Fold the front half of the cover toward the center.

3

PUSH

Push the entire folded cover back onto the lifter.

4

ENJOY

The cover rests securely—time to relax!

To close: Simply pull forward and unfold.

Safety Rules

  • ⏱ 15 Min Max: Limit soak time per session.
  • 👶 Supervision: Children under 16 must have an adult present.
  • 🚿 Shower First: Wash off sweat and body oils before entering.
  • 🧼 No Additives: Never use soaps, oils, or bubble bath in the tub.
  • 🚫 No Diving: Sitting and relaxing only.

Health & Warnings

  • 🍷 No Alcohol: No alcohol or drug use before or during soaking.
  • 🥂 No Glassware: Keep all glass away from the hot tub area.
  • 🤰 Medical Warning: Pregnant guests or those with mobility issues should consult a physician.
  • 🦶 Enter Clean: Thoroughly clean dirt and mud off your body before stepping in.
  • 🧺 Towel Warmer: Use the electric towel warmer bucket for a cozy exit!
