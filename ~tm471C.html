Fireplace & Gas Logs

STOP! If you smell gas, get everyone out and call Lance immediately! 770.549.9358

How to Start

Slowly turn the gas knob to 'ON'. Burner should light gently.

If Pilot is Out

Turn knob to 'PILOT' and push in. Insert lit match near back right prongs and hold for 30-60 seconds.

Main Floor Shower

It's a low-flow head and works differently than standard fixtures.

  1. 1. Turn on the water handles.
  2. 2. Adjust to your desired temperature.
  3. 3. Pull down the spout ring located right under the shower spout to engage the showerhead.

Retrowave Microwave

CRITICAL: Double-Cancel Safety Feature!

Pressing "PAUSE CANCEL" once only pauses the timer. You MUST press it a second time to clear the time and unlock the door!

Carefree Commode Care

  • 1. Use ONLY septic-safe TP provided.
  • 2. NO wipes, pads, or trash in the toilet.
  • 3. Give the handle a firm, complete flush.
  • 4. Wipe the seat if you sprinkle!
